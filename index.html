Charities

This year, we are supporting the following charity:

Clarissa Nicholls was a French and Italian student at Tit Hall, and would have been in her fourth and final year. She embodied the work hard play hard mindset. Her cackle was outrageously infectious, her positivity unwavering, her singing atrocious, and her love, ambition and achievements boundless.

Last year, Clarissa collapsed and passed away from an undiagnosed heart condition.
In cooperation with Cardiac Risk in the Young (CRY) Clarissa's family and the University, the campaign is hoping to start an annual four-day ECG screening service for Cambridge students.

We hope you will consider donating to the campaign to show your support here
